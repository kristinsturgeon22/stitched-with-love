Our Story

Welcome to Stitched with Love, where every stitch tells a story. We create handcrafted blankets, custom embroidery, and meaningful gifts designed to be treasured for generations.

Built on faith, family, and creativity, our mission is simple: to create pieces that become part of your family’s story.
