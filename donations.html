Our Community Mission

Wrap One. Gift One.

Creating heirlooms for our families while sharing warmth with residents at Sunrise Manor Nursing Home in Hodgenville, Kentucky.

How It Works

Every booking and purchase helps someone feel remembered.

One Workshop Booked

For every Heirloom Experience or workshop booked, Stitched with Love gifts one handmade blanket to a resident at Sunrise Manor.

One Blanket Purchased

Every blanket purchased automatically sponsors one donated blanket for a Sunrise Manor resident.

Choose the Recipient

Your sponsored blanket can honor someone you know.

1. Name a Resident

If you know someone who lives at Sunrise Manor, include their full name with your blanket request.

2. Leave It Open

If no resident is named, Sunrise Manor will choose the recipient for your sponsored blanket.

3. Share the Gift of Warmth

Your order or workshop helps remind a local resident that they are remembered and cared for.
