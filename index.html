Meet the Maker

Hi, I’m Kristin.

What began as a love for creating handmade gifts has grown into a faith-based business centered on craftsmanship, community, and creating pieces that become part of a family’s story.

Whether I am hand knitting a cozy blanket, creating a personalized keepsake, or hosting an Heirloom Experience, my prayer is that every piece brings comfort, joy, and lasting memories.

Thank you for supporting my dream and allowing me to create something meaningful for yours.

The Blanket Blessing Project

One Blanket Made. One Blanket Gifted. Twice the Love.

For every blanket purchased or created during an Heirloom Experience, Stitched with Love will gift a blanket to a resident at Sunrise Manor Nursing Home in Hodgenville, Kentucky.

When five guests create blankets, five blankets are gifted. Together, we share warmth and help someone in our community feel remembered.
